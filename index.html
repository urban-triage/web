Urban Triage has created a 10 week training course for Black parents and community members. The training includes but is not limited to social justice, organizing, self evaluation of behaviors, social skills, coping skills & tools, cultural heritage, advocacy, IEP’s, legal resources and processes as well as trauma informed care.

    Training Consist of:

  • Parent leadership development
  • Target Black parents and community members throughout Madison WI in collaboration with Madison Metropolitan School District, Sankofa Educational Leadership United and other organizations that share Urban Triage’s mission
  • Provide parent stipends (economic empowerment)
  • Professional & Personal Development
  • Advocacy & Social Justice Development
  • Individual Education Plan & disability rights training
  • Psycho education
  • Trauma informed care training
  • Parenting skills & tools
  • Analysis building
